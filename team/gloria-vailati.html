Gloria Vailati
Head of Marketing Connect

Gloria Vailati has more than 20 years of experience in the technology world through companies such as Compaq , HP and Apple and a strong trajectory in marketing and business management. She held positions such as Marketing Director, Internet Business Development Director for Compaq in Latin America, Director of PC´business for Hewlett Packard – a 1 billion business unit for the company- and at Apple she was General Manager for South America and the Caribbean. She also cofounded FWTV the first web TV platform in Latin America.

Gloria has a undergraduate degree in marketing communications from Universidad del Salvador in Buenos Aires, Argentina and an Executive MBA from the University of Houston.

She is member of Upward Women an organization aimed at helping women across the globe to step up to their potential.
