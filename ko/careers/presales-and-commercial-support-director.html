  • Constantly improve those processes and the skills of the team in pursuit of organizational excellence.
  • The target audiences are:
    • Developers (TBD with Innovation Studio)
    • Governments
    • Corporations
    • Partners
    • ISV
    • Solution Providers
    • Training Partners
    • Startups / Entrepreneurs
    • Social Impact Endeavors
  • Implement the systems that will support and measure the adoption cycles
  • Define and map the roles needed in all adoption projects
  • Make sure that all team members have and develop expert skills for their role

Experience & Skills Required

  • Own the adoption training program, make sure that contains are updated on a regular basis and that all adoption team members complete the whole adoption induction process
  • Facilitate the professional and personal growth and cultural alignment of all members of the Adoption Support area
  • Define and refine the Partners Program.
