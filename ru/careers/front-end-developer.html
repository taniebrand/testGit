About RIF OS:

Root Infrastructure Framework Open Standard (RIF OS) is a suite of open and decentralized infrastructure protocols that enable faster, easier and scalable development of distributed applications (dApps) within a unified environment.

RIF OS is an all in one, easy to use, blockchain infrastructure service framework that provides developers and organizations access to a variety of services across multiple crypto-economies.

RIF OS Protocols enable broad interoperability and faster time-to-deployment, and aim to bridge the gap between blockchain technologies and their mass-market adoption. RIF OS Protocols complete the RSK Smart Contract Network vision of bringing the Internet of Value to life.

What would you help to build:

Permissionless decentralised Directory Protocol (RDP) is the key technical component to tackle financial inclusion including:

  • 1 - Name Service - RIF Name Service (RNS) is a decentralised service that allows users to have a readable domain or alias in any blockchain. It can be used to identify other personal resources, such as payment, ID, storage or communication addresses.
  • 2 - Directory Services - Digital ID - Reputation - Intended to afford anyone in the world with an internet connection, regardless of location or means, the ability to store their digital identity, resources, and sensitive information, with the confidence that their data is cryptographically secure and private.

Anyone can participate in the network as a provider - listing their services on the marketplace. The system will integrate into enterprise ready framework providing P2P payment, storage, P2P messaging and data oracles.
